Welcome to Aqua World

At Aqua World, we invite you to journey beneath the waves and explore the vibrant, awe-inspiring world of marine life. From shimmering coral reefs to the mesmerizing depths of the deep blue, our aquarium offers an immersive experience that connects you to the heart of the ocean.

Jellyfish

Jellyfish are nature's living art forms. Their glowing bioluminescence and timeless elegance make them an oceanic wonder!

Seahorses

With delicate frames and horse-like appearance, seahorses symbolize oceanic elegance, with males birthing offspring.

Star Fish

Starfish are remarkable marine creatures that regenerate arms and maintain balance in the ocean's ecosystem.

Sea Pens

Sea pens are fascinating marine animals resembling quills, thriving in soft seabeds and playing a role in maintaining ocean ecosystems.

Coral Reef

Coral reefs are vibrant underwater ecosystems formed by tiny coral polyps. They support diverse marine life, protect coastlines, and are vital for ocean health.

Marine Mammals

Marine mammals, like whales, dolphins, and seals, are warm-blooded ocean animals vital to marine ecosystems.
